FOOTBALL

ABOUT:

I started playing football ate the age of 3 for my local team in Whitby, Western Suburbs.
I play in multiple positions with my best being Centre Attacking Midfield.
For my Wellington and National teams, I played right or left back and for school and seniors Striker, Winger or Centre Mid.

WELLINGTON AND NATIONAL:

For multiple years I played for the wellington federation team, competing in multiple national competitions, getting selected in the tournament team 2 out of 5 times.
I participated in multiple NTC’s (national talent centres) from the age of 13-16 but could never get a solid grip and retain my place in the NTC squad which would’ve given me a shot of making the National Youth Teams (u17s and 19s).

SCHOOL:

I started playing u15s at HIBS, got called up to first team in year 11 but refused to play due to having other football commitments (senior). I also didn’t play through year 12 because of this.
I came back in year 13 as it was my last year. after getting into a row with the coach I decided to play second team.
We made history by becoming first HIBS second team to play in division 2. Finished 2nd to last but had several good points including beating the HIBS first team in a tournament game.
I ended the season getting four awards being Player of the year, Players player of the year, Top goal scorer and Best Attitude.

SENIOR:

Joining when I was 15 years old I got thrown straight into the first team setup but mainly playing with the reserves, I made 4 appearances for the 1st team that season playing in the top division in wellington.
